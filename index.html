I've worked on some of the largest and most performant Rails apps including:

During tax peak this was an incredible amount of requests. Downtime meant extremely degraded Turbo Tax experience and hundreds of millions in lost revenue. Multi-az and multi-region required when tooling for this was non-existent at the time requiring creative engineering solutions.
